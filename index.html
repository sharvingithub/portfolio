Know about Me

Meet Sharvin Thomas, the creative powerhouse behind some of Ottawa's coolest local brand designs. Hailing from India, Sharvin infuses his unique cultural perspective into his work, resulting in eye-catching designs that perfectly capture the spirit of Ottawa's diverse community. With his trusty Mac and a never-ending supply of coffee, Sharvin's passion for design shines through in every project he takes on. Whether he's working on a logo for a new startup or creating packaging for a local artisanal food brand, Sharvin's designs are always fresh, fun, and full of personality.

When Sharvin is not busy designing, you can find him exploring the great outdoors. Nature is a huge inspiration for him, and he finds that being surrounded by the beauty of the world helps him to think more creatively. From hiking in the nearby Gatineau Hills to kayaking along the Rideau Canal, Sharvin loves to immerse himself in nature and draw inspiration from its colors, textures, and patterns. He believes that a deep connection with the environment is essential for creating designs that truly resonate with people, and it's this passion that sets him apart in the world of graphic design.
